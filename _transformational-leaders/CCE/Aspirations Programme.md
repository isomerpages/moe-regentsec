<p>Regent’s Aspirations programme adopts the <strong>4-D approach</strong> to
education and career guidance: Discover, Decide, Develop and Dream. Through
a 4/5-year developmental programme, students’ awareness of their strengths
and passions are enhanced, and guidance given in their pursuits of their
aspired educational pathways, courses and careers.</p>
<p>Our cohort programmes:</p>
<p><strong>Secondary One: </strong>
</p>
<p>Customised ECGC Group Counselling with ECG Counsellor and ECG learning
journeys to discover individual's values, interests, strengths and personalities.</p>
<p><strong>Secondary Two:</strong>
</p>
<p>Exposure to different Worlds of Tomorrow through taster experiences and
career exploration festival.</p>
<p><strong>Upper Secondary Students: RE<em>gent</em> Discovers Aspirations</strong>
</p>
<p>Our signature Start-of-Year programme comprises various rich learning
experiences, including: :</p>
<p>(a) exposure to various post-secondary institutions including polytechnics,
ITE and junior colleges through learning journeys, workshops and informational
booths</p>
<p>(b) exposure to various industries through learning journeys to the world
of work</p>
<p>(c) exposure to various jobs through invited guest speakers from various
professions and industries</p>
