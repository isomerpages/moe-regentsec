<p><strong>Principal’s Address</strong>
</p>
<p>Welcome to Regent Secondary School! As you navigate our website, I hope
you catch a glimpse of the vibrant energy and "blazing spirit" that defines
our school community. At Regent, we believe every student (Regenite) possesses
the potential to shine, and it is our privilege to ignite that passion
for lifelong learning and excellence.</p>
<p>The school vision&nbsp;is to nurture Regenites into<em> </em><strong><em>Self-Directed Learners</em></strong>, <strong><em>Confident Leaders</em></strong> and <strong><em>in Pursuit of Excellence</em></strong>.&nbsp;
This vision is anchored by the school mission, ‘<strong><em>Realising the Potential of Our Students’</em></strong><em> </em>underpinned
by school’s values<em>: </em><strong>PRIDE – P</strong>assion,&nbsp;<strong>R</strong>espect,&nbsp;<strong>I</strong>ntegrity,&nbsp;<strong>D</strong>etermination,
and&nbsp;<strong>E</strong>mpathy. These values serve as the moral compass
for our students, ensuring they grow not just in intellect, but in character.
Our school&nbsp;<strong>Motto</strong>,&nbsp;<strong>"Blaze Forth to Glory,"</strong>&nbsp;reminds
us daily to strive for greatness with an undaunting spirit.</p>
<p>At Regent, education is a partnership between school, home, and the community.
Our students can do their best when given the space and time to learn and
grow and their parents work with the school in a respectful and trusting
approach.</p>
<p><strong>Mrs Sng Siew Hong</strong>
<br>Principal, Regent Secondary School</p>
