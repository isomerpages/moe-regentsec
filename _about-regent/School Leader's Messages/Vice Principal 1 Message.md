<p><strong>Vice-Principal’s Message</strong>
<br>
<br><strong>“What will I be in the future?”</strong> Young minds often ask
this question of their parents, teachers, and friends as they journey through
the milestones of life.
<br>
<br>At Regent Secondary School, our main aim is to build character and equip
students with the skills and mindset needed for the future. We provide
a nurturing environment where universal values, practical skillsets, and
critical thinking are developed to prepare our Regenites for what lies
ahead. Guided by our<strong> PRIDE values: P</strong>assion,&nbsp;<strong>R</strong>espect,&nbsp;<strong>I</strong>ntegrity,&nbsp;<strong>D</strong>etermination,
and&nbsp;<strong>E</strong>mpathy —students are equipped with a strong

<br>

and kindness. Every educator at Regent is committed to bringing warmth
and hope to each young heart and mind that passes through our gates.
<br>
<br><strong>Striving for a future that’s ours to embrace,</strong>
<br>Together with you.

<br><strong>Mdm Ng Tze Fang</strong>
