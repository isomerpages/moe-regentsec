<p><strong>Principal’s Address</strong>
</p>
<p>Welcome to Regent Secondary School! As you navigate our website, I hope
you catch a glimpse of the vibrant energy and "blazing spirit" that defines
our school community. At Regent, we believe every student (Regenite) possesses
the potential to shine, and it is our privilege to ignite that passion
for lifelong learning and excellence.</p>
<p>The school vision&nbsp;is to nurture Regenites into<em> </em><strong><em>Self-Directed Learners</em></strong>, <strong><em>Confident Leaders</em></strong> and <strong><em>in Pursuit of Excellence</em></strong>.&nbsp;
This vision is anchored by the school mission, ‘<strong><em>Realising the Potential of Our Students’</em></strong><em> </em>underpinned
by school’s values<em>: </em><strong>PRIDE – P</strong>assion,&nbsp;<strong>R</strong>espect,&nbsp;<strong>I</strong>ntegrity,&nbsp;<strong>D</strong>etermination,
and&nbsp;<strong>E</strong>mpathy. These values serve as the moral compass
for our students, ensuring they grow not just in intellect, but in character.
Our school&nbsp;<strong>Motto</strong>,&nbsp;<strong>"Blaze Forth to Glory,"</strong>&nbsp;reminds
us daily to strive for greatness with an undaunting spirit.</p>
<p>At Regent, education is a partnership between school, home, and the community.
Our students can do their best when given the space and time to learn and
grow and their parents work with the school in a respectful and trusting
approach.</p>
<p><strong>Mrs Sng Siew Hong</strong>
<br>Principal, Regent Secondary School
<br>

<br>We are an inclusive and caring community where Regenites learn with grace
and kindness. Every educator at Regent is committed to bringing warmth
and hope to each young heart and mind that passes through our gates.
<br>
<br><strong>Striving for a future that’s ours to embrace,</strong>

<p><strong>Vice-Principal’s Address</strong>
<br>
<br>In Regent Secondary School, every student is a unique story of growth
and development to be told. Our commitment is to provide the inspiration
and the nurturing support for the stories to unfold.
<br>
<br>One voice, one mind,
<br>
<br>We will blaze forth to glory
<br>
<br>The above lines from our school song ‘Blaze Forth to Glory’ echoes the
determination we nurture in our students so that they pursue their areas
of excellence and serve with love and honour as Confident Leaders.
<br>
<br>We strongly believe that true success is measured by our students’ character
and their ability to navigate the world with sound moral values. This theme
binds every story. In a world of rapid technological shifts and global
complexity, we also aim to provide our students with the education that
transcend conventional boundaries of textbooks to authentic learning experiences
that strengthen the development of an inquisitive mind, a sense of community
and an appreciation for lifelong learning as Self-Directed Learners. This
enables students to develop the might to make a positive difference and
gives each story its meaningful chapters.
<br>
<br>The village of home-school and community partnership working hand-in-hand
gives our students’ stories the musicality. In this partnership, we synergise
with one voice, one mind in nurturing the students’ character and we pool
our varied expertise and resources to enable the stories to come to life
and to resonate with all. We build common grounds and explore new horizons.
<br>
<br>United in Purpose Always to Our Next Chapter …
<br>
<br><strong>Mrs Dalina A. Firdouse</strong>
<br>Vice-Principal, Regent Secondary School</p>
