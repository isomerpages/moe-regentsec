<h4>Vision</h4>
<p>Students are passionate about the arts and able to create and appreciate
different dance forms.</p>
<h4>Mission</h4>
<p>Spread knowledge and appreciation of dance to the school and community
as passionate advocates.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/CCA/Modern Dance/MODDAN2024_1.jpg">
</div>
<p>One of the most famous lines in the film Ratatouille is that ‚Äúanyone can
cook‚Äù. In Regent Modern Dance, we believe that anyone can dance. We welcome
all who are interested in dance and challenge every member to grow and
achieve their personal goals in their dance journey.</p>
<p>As part of the Regent Modern Dance holistic experience, our students learn
what it means to develop and grow as a team through rigorous training and
bonding activities. They build confidence through numerous performance
opportunities at school, community, and national events, including the
Regent25 Awards Day (2025), Limbang National Day Carnival (2025) and the
SYF Arts Presentation (2025), where we are proud to have achieved the Certificate
of Distinction.</p>
<p>In our CCA, we nurture not only an appreciation for the arts but also
essential values such as resilience, discipline and camaraderie. Together,
we celebrate our milestones, overcome challenges, and continue to grow
as one united team.</p>
<p>With the support of our school leaders, teachers-in-charge, and instructor,
Regent Modern Dance looks forward to growing confident and expressive dancers
by providing our members with opportunities to lead, learn and perform.</p>
<p>In Regent Modern Dance, we truly believe that anyone can dance. It is
not talent alone, but passion and determination, that shine brightest on
stage.</p>
<p>As our motto so aptly goes,&nbsp;<em>‚ÄúDance with your heart, and your feet will follow‚Äù.</em>
</p>
