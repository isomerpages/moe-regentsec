<h4>Vision</h4>
<p>Every player a dedicated sportsperson and competent leader.</p>
<h4>Mission</h4>
<p>Every member to demonstrate good behaviour and character, and to put in
their best effort during every training session. Each member will strive
to demonstrate good leadership qualities and be an active contributor back
to the community.</p>

<p>The Regent Football Club aims to nurture a team of dedicated footballers
who strive for excellence and uphold the values of good sportsmanship on
and off the pitch.</p>
<p>Beyond the football pitch, our footballers are also given the opportunity
to actively contribute to the community through CCA-initiated Values-in-Action
(VIA) projects.</p>
