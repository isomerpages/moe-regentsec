<h4>Vision</h4>
<p>To develop Regenites to be a future-ready and responsible digital learner.</p>
<h4>Mission</h4>
<p>To cultivate the students’ passion for different forms of Digital Media
and to train them to be technology savvy and competent in the use of audio
and visual equipment</p>
<h4>About Us</h4>
<p><strong>Sound, lights, cameras, ACTION!<br></strong>At <strong>Regent Infocomm Technology (Media Production)</strong>,
we bring creativity and storytelling to life through digital media. Our
members gain hands-on experience in photography, videography, and audio-visual
technology, learning to operate professional equipment and manage live
productions.</p>
<p>Beyond capturing moments, our team plays a vital role in supporting school
events and activities — from setting up sound systems and managing lighting
to filming performances and ceremonies. Through these experiences, students
develop teamwork, creativity, and confidence — essential skills for the
digital age.</p>
