<p>On 19 May 2026, Regenites gathered for the Student Leaders’ Investiture,
a significant occasion celebrating the spirit of service, leadership and
community in Regent Secondary School. Centred on the theme, <em>“To Serve, To Lead, To Transform.”</em>,
the ceremony marked both the appointment of incoming student leaders and
a tribute to those who had faithfully served the school community.</p>
<p>The event began with the presentation of badges to Class Chairpersons,
Vice-Chairpersons and Peer Support Leaders, recognising the responsibilities
they will undertake in building a positive and supportive school environment
grounded in respect, empathy and integrity.</p>
<p>Excitement filled the hall as introduction videos and walk-ins by the
Student Council and CCA Leaders showcased the passion and commitment of
the newly appointed leaders. Warm applause from fellow Regenites reflected
the collective pride and encouragement of the school community.</p>
<p>A key highlight of the ceremony was the presentation of Letters of Appointment
by the School Leaders, affirming the trust placed in the incoming student
leaders to serve with courage and purpose. Certificates of Appreciation
were also presented to the outgoing Presidents of the 15th Student Council
and CCA Leaders in recognition of their dedication and contributions.</p>
<p>The symbolic handover of leadership by the outgoing leaders to their successors
marked the passing of responsibility, trust and commitment to serve the
school community. The ceremony concluded with the Student Leaders’ Pledge
as student leaders committed themselves to serve with empathy, tenacity
and purpose.</p>
