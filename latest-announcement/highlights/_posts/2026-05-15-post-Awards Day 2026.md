<p>Awards Day 2026, themed ‘In Pursuit of Excellence’, was a meaningful celebration
of the achievements, growth and aspirations of both our past and present
Regenites.</p>
<p>More than a recognition of academic accomplishments, the event honoured
the resilience and dedication demonstrated by our students across the many
areas of school life. It also celebrated the unwavering support of the
school’s partners, whose encouragement and contributions continue to strengthen
our shared mission of nurturing every student holistically and empowering
them to reach their fullest potential.</p>
<p>We were deeply honoured to welcome our Guest-of-Honour, Mr Low Chin Kok,
whose inspiring address reminded students that excellence is not simply
measured by achievements, but by the courage to persevere and the determination
to overcome challenges. His words encouraged both award recipients and
the student audience to pursue their goals with resilience, purpose and
confidence.</p>
<p>Adding a heartfelt touch to the celebration, our valedictorian, Aidan
Goh, shared his memorable journey at Regent Secondary School. Through his
personal sharing on how he overcame the different obstacles he encountered,
he inspired his peers to embrace challenges, believe in their potential
and continue striving for excellence in all that they do.</p>
<p>Well done, Regenites!</p>
