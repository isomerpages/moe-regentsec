<p>On 18 May, Regent Secondary celebrated the Ray of Regent nominees for
Semester 1, recognising students who have exemplified the school's core
values of passion, respect, integrity, determination and empathy.</p>
<p>The Ray of Regent award affirms students whose actions have made a positive
difference to those around them. Nominated by their peers and teachers,
these students consistently demonstrate outstanding character through their
everyday actions. Whether providing emotional and academic support to classmates,
taking the initiative to maintain a conducive learning environment, displaying
exceptional sportsmanship during competitions, or lending a helping hand
to members of the public, they embody the values that define the Regent
spirit.</p>
<p>The atmosphere was filled with excitement as nominees were recognised
in front of their peers. Loud cheers and enthusiastic applause echoed through
the hall as students received a certificate of recognition and a star plushie
keychain. The celebration reflected the pride of the Regent community in
acknowledging students who have led by example and made a meaningful difference
in the lives of others.</p>
<p>More than a recognition of individual achievements, the award celebrates
the impact that students can have on their school and wider community through
acts of service, leadership and kindness. Congratulations to all our Ray
of Regent nominees! May their actions continue to inspire fellow Regenites
to serve, lead and transform with purpose and heart.</p>
