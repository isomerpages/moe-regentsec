<p>On 18 May, our school came together for the CCA Awards Presentation to
celebrate the outstanding achievements of our CCAs in Semester One. The
event recognised the dedication, perseverance and commitment of our student
awardees, who were honoured in front of their peers for their accomplishments
and contributions.</p>
<p>We extend our heartfelt congratulations to the following CCAs:</p>
<p><strong>Uniformed Groups</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Boys’ Brigade – JM Fraser Unit Award (High Distinction Award)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Boys’ Brigade – Blaze 2026 (Gold Honour Roll and 3rd Place, Open Category)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Girls’ Brigade – Company Award (Gold Award)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>National Police Cadet Corps – Unit Overall Proficiency Award (Distinction
Award)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Red Cross Youth – Unit Award (Gold Award)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Clubs and Societies</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Environmental Science Club – Singapore Environment Council School Green
Award (Vanda Miss Joaquim Award)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Sports and Games</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Football (C Division) – National School Games Championship 2025 Fair Play
Award</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Netball (B Division) – 3rd Place in West Zone and Top 8 in Nationals</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Taekwondo – National School Games Poomsae &amp; Kyorugi (2 Silver and
3 Bronze Medals)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>We are proud of our students’ achievements and look forward to seeing
our Regenites continue to blaze forth to greater heights.</p>
