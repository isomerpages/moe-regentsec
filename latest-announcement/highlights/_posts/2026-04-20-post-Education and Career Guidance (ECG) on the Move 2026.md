<p>From 30 March 2026 to 2 April 2026, several Education and Career Guidance
(ECG) booths and panels were set up next to the canteen in the school.
These encouraged the students to explore and participate in an immersive
ECG experience during their recess and lunch breaks. The programme aimed
to provide students with opportunities to explore and reassess their interests,
skills, and strengths related to the different post-secondary academic
options and the industries they are interested in learning more about.
The three-day in-house programme also allowed students to explore job roles
from the listed industries and gain a preliminary idea of the career types
that best suit them. Hence, the programme enabled them to discover potential
career options and explore opportunities that may be relevant to these
options.</p>
<p>ECG on the Move was mainly facilitated by the Character and Citizenship
Education (CCE) Representatives from each class who brought their classmates
to the exhibits and facilitated the sessions. The school’s ECG counsellor,
also assisted the CCE Representatives and clarified any doubts that the
students might have.</p>
<p>The experience in participating in ECG on the Move Programme has certainly
enlightened our students on some possible post-secondary educational pathways
and ignited their passion to pursue their dream courses in the post-secondary
institution of their choice.
