<p>On 11 May, our Secondary Four Normal (Academic) and Normal (Technical)
students visited the Institute of Technical Education (ITE) College West
to gain deeper insight into the various courses that are offered by the
institute as well as the entry requirements for each course. Through this
visit, our Secondary Four students had a greater understanding of ITE educational
pathways, and the overall post-secondary educational experience offered
by ITEs in general. In addition, the visit also included an Early Admissions
Exercise (EAE) Workshop which shared the necessary steps that the students
had to take when they create their EAE portfolios and prepare for their
EAE interviews.</p>
<p>The ‘Come Visit ITE College West Learning Journey’ has definitely provided
greater clarity to our Secondary Four Normal (Academic) and Normal (Technical)
students on the possible post-secondary educational pathways through the
ITE and supported their readiness for the EAE application period in June.</p>
