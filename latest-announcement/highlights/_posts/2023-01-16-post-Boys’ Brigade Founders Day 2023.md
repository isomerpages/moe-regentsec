<p>The Boys' Brigade (BB) celebrated their BB Day on 12th January annually
to commemorate the founding of The Boys' Brigade in Singapore. Today, Regent’s
BB Boys don their uniform proudly and commemorates the event with a parade
for the new Secondary One students at the school parade square. Students
in the classrooms were treated to a video montage prepared by the BB 91st
Company for them to understand more about the uniformed group as well as
the wide range of activities organized.</p>
