<p><strong><u>CNY Inter-Class Domino challenge 2026</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Regenites ushered in the 2026 Chinese New Year with colour, energy, and
creativity through an exciting inter-class Domino competition that brought
teamwork and imagination to life. Working hand in hand, each class designed
and built intricate domino runs inspired by Lunar New Year themes such
as prosperity, renewal, and unity — turning traditional symbols into moving
visual displays.</p>
<p>The journey was just as meaningful as the final topple. Beyond the excitement
of the moment itself, the process strengthened bonds as students planned,
problem-solved, and supported one another every step of the way. Adding
deeper significance to the celebration, many classes also incorporated
recycled and reused materials into their designs, serving as a timely reminder
of our shared environmental responsibility.</p>
<p>The event was a joyful reminder that celebrating cultural traditions can
also inspire teamwork, creativity, and greater environmental awareness.</p>
